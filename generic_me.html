About Me

I came into existence on 2nd of November 1999 in the town of Vikasnagar in outskirts of Dehradun city in the state of Uttarakhand. I completed my schooling up to class 12th, or you may say Intermediate, in Vikasnagar itself.

I have always been a big fan of music and I have been singing since I was five. I have been performing at school functions for all my school years and after joining college I represented my hostel in music competitions.

I come from a middle-class family. In my family, I have my parents, a brother and a sister. My friend circle is called Kasukabe Defence Group. I like to think of myself as a calm person with a lot of interests, sometimes makes me unclear, and a belief in goodness. This may seem a little old fashioned but I believe in hard work.
